Watch Bea in Action!

Step into the life of Bea, a young single mother trying to survive during the COVID-19 pandemic. While caring for her newborn baby, Bea must manage limited resources, complete daily tasks, and keep her family safe. As the game progresses, new responsibilities appear, including taking care of her grandmother living with Dementia and supporting her younger brother. Can you help Bea overcome every challenge and keep her family together?
